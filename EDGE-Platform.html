Golden Rule: 5+ metrics pointing the same direction = highest-confidence play. Public Betting % (Metric 08) is most powerful when it CONTRADICTS the other 8 — that's where sharp money and model edge align.
METRIC 01 · HIGH WEIGHT
3-Point Percentage
SPREAD + TOTAL
Predicts scoring variance and upset potential. Teams that control the arc control the pace of scoring. A team with elite 3PT% offense vs. a team that can't defend the arc is a recipe for an over.
Elite Offense: >38% | Elite Defense: <28% allowed
Flag: >5% gap between teams
→ Find at: KenPom, ESPN, Sports Reference
METRIC 02 · HIGH WEIGHT
Win % in Context
SPREAD + TOTAL
Home/away record vs. similar opponents. A 15-2 team that is 1-4 on the road vs. ranked opponents is very different from what their record suggests. Recency matters.
Road dog >60% road win rate = value
Home fav <50% home win rate = fade
→ Find at: ESPN, Sports Reference schedule pages
METRIC 03 · HIGH WEIGHT
Offensive Rebound Rate
SPREAD + TOTAL
Second-chance points are among the most reliable predictors of over/under outcomes. High OffReb% teams consistently generate extra possessions that inflate scoring.
>35% OffReb = significant second-chance threat
<25% = limited extra possessions
→ Find at: KenPom, Sports Reference
METRIC 04 · HIGH WEIGHT
Defensive Efficiency
SPREAD + TOTAL
Points allowed per 100 possessions, adjusted for opponent strength. The single best predictor of low-scoring games and consistent covers. Elite D beats elite O in close games.
Top 30 nationally = elite anchor
Rank 150+ = exploitable | 50+ point gap = strong lean
→ Find at: KenPom adjusted defensive efficiency
METRIC 05 · MED-HIGH WEIGHT · NEW
Pace & Tempo
TOTALS
Possessions per 40 minutes. Tempo mismatches are consistently undervalued by the market. The slowest team almost always sets the pace — fast team visiting slow home team = UNDER lean.
>72 possessions = fast | <65 = slow
Gap >5 = significant mismatch → factor into totals
→ Find at: KenPom Adj Tempo column
METRIC 06 · MEDIUM WEIGHT · NEW
Free Throw Percentage
SPREAD
Clutch games are won at the free throw line. Teams shooting below 65% FT% are a fade in close games. Becomes most important metric in the final 4 minutes of tight spreads.
>80% = reliable closer | <65% = fade tight spreads
High rate (>25 attempts/game) = lean toward over
→ Find at: ESPN, Sports Reference team stats
METRIC 07 · MEDIUM WEIGHT · NEW
Bench Depth
SPREAD
Points per game from bench/non-starters. Depth matters in foul trouble, back-to-backs, and rivalry games. Shallow benches collapse under late-game pressure.
>20 bench PPG = deep roster
<10 bench PPG = starter-dependent (vulnerable in foul trouble)
→ Find at: ESPN team stats pages
METRIC 08 · MED-HIGH WEIGHT · NEW
Public Betting % — Sharp Signal
ALL BETS
When the line moves AGAINST the public betting %, sharp/professional money is on the other side. That's where the value lives. Most powerful when it contradicts the other 8 metrics.
>70% public on one side = look at the other side
Reverse line movement = sharp money signal
→ Find at: Action Network, ESPN BET
